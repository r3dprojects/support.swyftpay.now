# Support

## Getting help

| I need help with... | Where to go |
|---|---|
| Using swyftpay.now | [faq.swyftec.com](https://faq.swyftec.com) |
| Current platform status | [status.swyftec.com](https://status.swyftec.com) |
| A bug or unexpected behaviour | [Open a Bug Report](https://github.com/r3dprojects/support.swyftpay.now/issues/new?template=1-bug-report.yml) |
| Requesting a new feature | [Open a Feature Request](https://github.com/r3dprojects/support.swyftpay.now/issues/new?template=2-feature-request.yml) |
| Something account-related or sensitive | [info@swyftec.com](mailto:info@swyftec.com) |

## Response times

We are a small team. We aim to triage public issues within 3 business days and respond to email within 2 business days. We cannot guarantee resolution timelines for every issue.
